What is Loop?
A convenience product for every table.

Loop helps customers, ground staff and brand owners have a smoother dining experience — all through a single QR code on the table.

👤

For Customers

Seamless dining experience through 1 QR scan — menu, ordering, payment, tips and reviews.

🧑‍🍳

For Ground Staff

Increases turnaround time while boosting daily tips — more service, less payment friction.

🏢

For Brand Owners

Reduces transaction costs while streamlining all transactions via POS integration.

The Pitch Script
The Script

"This is an end-to-end convenience product — from scanning the code on your table to ensuring they return."

1

Customer arrives and settles at their table.

2

They scan the QR on the table to view the menu — opens a webpage on their phone.

3

They view the menu and place the order (in-app or with staff).

4

Done eating — scan QR again → click Pay Bill.

5

Staff concludes the KOT on POS as normal.

It is that simple!

Don't Pitch Gold + Loop Together

The confusion can lead to you losing both deals. Keep them completely separate pitches at all times.

The Right Order

During your Gold pitch, inquire about their current POS & payment gateway. Once Gold is live, connect again to pitch Loop as the logical next step.

Daily Field Operations

Objectives · Mission · Daily goals for the Loop Ops Team.

Field Objectives
Objectives, Mission & Goal
OBJ

Objectives

Evaluate how effectively merchants and customers are adopting Loop QR as a daily payment solution — and ensure merchants fully understand the value of Loop QR and how it contributes to their business growth.

MSN

Mission

Empower merchants to become self-sufficient Loop QR champions through consistent field engagement, retrainings, operational support and partnership — creating a seamless digital payment experience that drives value for businesses, staff and customers alike.

GRL

Daily Goal

Actively support and encourage staff to complete 1–3 successful Loop QR transactions on every visit — targeting 2–3 outlets visited per day, as consistently as possible.

Field Activities
How We Operate in the Field
🕵️

Mystery Shopping

Unannounced visits to experience the actual customer journey. 2–3 outlets per day.

✅Staff confidence in promoting Loop QR
✅Customer adoption & payment behavior
✅Transaction flow integrity
✅Merchant readiness & service quality
